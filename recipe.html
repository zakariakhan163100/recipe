RECIPE OF BIRYANI:

Ingredients:

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 4 cups water
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • 4-5 green cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 cloves
  • Salt to taste

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves, chopped

For the Biryani Layers:

  • 3 large onions, thinly sliced and fried until golden brown
  • 1/2 cup ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1 cup fried cashews and raisins (optional)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves, chopped

For Biryani Masala:

  • 2 teaspoons cumin seeds
  • 4-5 green cardamom pods
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves

Instructions:

  • Rinse the soaked basmati rice under running water until the water runs clear. In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the soaked and drained rice along with bay leaves, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, cloves, and salt. Cook until the rice is 70-80% cooked. Drain the water and set aside.
  • In a bowl, mix the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, and chopped coriander and mint leaves. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  • In a separate pan, heat ghee or vegetable oil. Add the cumin seeds, green cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, and bay leaves for the biryani masala. Sauté until the spices release their aroma.
  • Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until the chicken is browned and cooked through.
  • In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, layer half of the partially cooked rice. Top it with the cooked chicken and sprinkle with fried onions, cashews, raisins, and chopped coriander and mint leaves. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of rice on top.
  • Drizzle saffron-infused milk or food color mixed with milk over the top layer of rice. You can also add a few teaspoons of ghee.
  • Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes, allowing the biryani to steam and the flavors to meld.
  • Once done, gently fluff the biryani with a fork. Serve hot with raita or a side salad.

Enjoy your delicious homemade chicken biryani!

KABLI PULAO RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 lb (450g) lamb or beef, cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped almonds or slivered pistachios
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups chicken or beef broth

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the Rice:
    • Rinse the Basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
    • Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Prepare the Meat:
    • n a large pot, heat oil or ghee over medium heat.
    • Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
    • Add meat chunks and brown them on all sides.
    • Season with salt, pepper, cumin seeds, cardamom powder, and cinnamon powder.
  3. Cook the Rice:
    • Add the soaked and drained rice to the pot with meat.
    • Stir well to coat the rice with the spices.
    • Pour in the chicken or beef broth.
    • Bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  4. Prepare the Garnishes:
    • In a separate pan, sauté shredded carrots until they're slightly caramelized.
    • In the same pan, lightly toast the almonds or pistachios.
    • Add raisins to the pan and let them plump up.
  5. Assemble the Kabuli Pulao:
    • Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork.
    • Gently mix in the caramelized carrots, toasted nuts, and plumped raisins.
    • Adjust seasoning if necessary.
  6. Serve:
    • Transfer the Kabuli Pulao to a serving platter.
    • Arrange the meat chunks on top.
    • Garnish with additional nuts, raisins, and chopped fresh herbs if desired.

Enjoy your flavorful Kabuli Pulao!
